Sources: Energy Institute Statistical Review of World Energy 2025 · IEA Global Energy Review 2025 · IMF WEO · World Bank (2024 data)

Primary energy measures total energy content of fuels before conversion, including heat lost in power generation. Final energy (total final consumption) is what end-users actually consume in industry, transport, and buildings. Globally, final energy is roughly 65-70% of primary energy; the exact ratio varies by country.
